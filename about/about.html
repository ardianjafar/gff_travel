our history

Good Foundation Forming was established in 2021. For the first time in Mongolia, a training support center has been established in cooperation with the International American University located in Los Angeles, California.This opportunity is not only limited to learning from home, but also provides you with the advice, information and resources you need to succeed from a training camp in Mongolia.


We are excited to invite Mongolians to explore and experience the beauty of island life this year. Our goal is to showcase the diversity and appeal of island tourism, making it accessible to people from all corners of the world. In addition to cultural immersion, we aim to enhance our students' English language skills through a study abroad program in Bali.

vision and mission

Our mission this year is to facilitate cultural exchange by introducing Mongolians to the allure of island nations. We aim to provide a deeper understanding of island tourism and its global significance.

  • Our mission is to be the world's premier provider of customized and unforgettable travel experiences, fostering a global community of learners dedicated to positive social impact.

  • We strive to create unforgettable memories for our clients by offering personalized travel itineraries, expert guidance, and exceptional service.

  • We believe in the transformative power of travel and are dedicated to providing sustainable and authentic travel experiences that enrich lives and contribute to the well-being of local communities.
